# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This is a technical knowledge base. Contributions and discussions should stay focused, accurate, and respectful.

In short:
- Be direct and constructive
- Disagree with ideas, not people
- No harassment, discrimination, or bad-faith behavior

## Scope

Applies to all repo interactions — issues, PRs, discussions, and any community spaces linked from this project.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to the maintainer via GitHub's private messaging or by emailing through the profile. Reports will be handled discreetly.

Consequences range from a warning to a permanent ban depending on severity.
